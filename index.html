Bonus Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between sticky and cashable bonuses?

Sticky bonuses remain part of the casino balance and are removed after wagering, while cashable bonuses become real money you can withdraw after meeting requirements.

How do wagering requirements work?

You must bet the bonus amount a set number of times before withdrawing. A 30x requirement on a $100 bonus means $3,000 in total bets.

Can I claim multiple bonuses at once?

Generally no - most casinos require you to complete one bonus's wagering before claiming another, but some offer parallel bonus systems.

What games contribute most to wagering?

Slots usually contribute 100%, while table games and live casino often contribute much less (10-20%) or nothing at all.
